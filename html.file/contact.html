Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my child’s first visit?

Please bring any previous medical records, vaccination history, and recent test results if available. This helps the doctor understand your child’s condition better.

Is it necessary to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, booking an appointment is recommended to ensure timely consultation and reduce waiting time for your child.

How should I prepare my child for a clinic visit?

Try to explain the visit in a calm and simple way. Reassure your child that the doctor is there to help and keep them healthy.

Can parents stay with their child during examination?

Yes, parents are encouraged to stay with their child during the consultation for comfort and to better understand the doctor’s instructions.

What if my child is nervous or afraid of doctors?

Our clinic is designed to be child-friendly, and our staff uses a gentle approach to help children feel safe and relaxed during their visit.

What happens if follow-up care is needed?

If follow-up visits are required, the doctor will clearly explain the plan, schedule, and any necessary instructions for continued care.
